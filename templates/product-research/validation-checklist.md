## ✅ Validation Criteria

### 1. Monthly Sales Volume

- Actual: _____ units/month

---

### 2. Price Point
- [ ] **Target: $15–75**
- Actual selling price: $_____
- **Why this matters:** Too low = low margins. Too high = harder to impulse buy.
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

### 3. Weight & Size
- [ ] **Target: <2 lbs, standard size**
- Actual weight: _____ lbs
- Dimensions: _____ x _____ x _____
- **Why this matters:** Lightweight = lower FBA fees ($2.50 vs $6+)
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

### 4. Top Seller Review Count
- [ ] **Target: <500 reviews on top seller**
- Top seller reviews: _____
- **Why this matters:** <500 = easier to compete. >2,000 = saturated.
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

### 5. Top 3 Seller Dominance
- [ ] **Target: Top 3 sellers control <70% of sales**
- Top 3 seller market share: _____%
- **Why this matters:** >70% = oligopoly, hard to break in.
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

### 6. Net Profit Margin
- [ ] **Target: >25% after ALL fees**
- Selling price: $_____
- Product cost: $_____
- Amazon fees (referral + FBA + storage): $_____
- Ad cost (assume 25% ACoS): $_____
- **Net profit:** $_____
- **Net margin:** _____%
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

### 7. Price Stability (12 Months)
- [ ] **Target: <20% monthly price fluctuations**
- Use Keepa to check price history
- **Observations:**  
  - Stable / Volatile
  - If volatile, why? (seasonal, price wars, new competitors)
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

### 8. Seasonality
- [ ] **Target: Year-round demand OR intentional seasonal launch**
- Peak months: _____
- Off-season months: _____
- Sales drop in off-season: _____%
- **Why this matters:** Avoid 100% seasonal unless you plan for it.
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

### 9. Category Restrictions & Policy Risks
- [ ] **Target: NOT in a gated category**
- Category: _____
- Gated? Yes / No
- Brand restrictions? Yes / No
- Any IP concerns? Yes / No
- **Status:** PASS / FAIL

---

## Final Validation Score

**Criteria Passed:** _____ / 9

### Decision Matrix:
- **9/9:** ✅ EXCELLENT – Proceed immediately
- **7-8/9:** ✅ GOOD – Proceed with minor cautions
- **5-6/9:** ⚠️ MARGINAL – Research more or improve sourcing
- **<5/9:** ❌ REJECT – Does not meet criteria

---

## Red Flags (Check any that apply)

- [ ] Gated category (Beauty, Baby, Health, Jewelry)
- [ ] Brand restrictions (Nike, Apple, Samsung, etc.)
- [ ] Fragile or hazardous (high return rate risk)
- [ ] Complex electronics (high support burden)
- [ ] Extreme seasonality (>75% sales in one quarter)
- [ ] Price wars visible in Keepa chart
- [ ] Top 3 sellers are Amazon or major brands

**If 2+ red flags are checked, strongly reconsider.**
